Here's the deal

So you're not actually a suspect. However, we are currently investigating the matter and need your help.

Last night, at approximately 5:30 PM, an assortment of guests arrived at Pendragon House in Lisworthy for a dinner party. Among those attending were Jerry Sorielli, Solomon McFlurry, Eliza Mirel, Anabell Jorgunsen, and Yorick Littles. They met the host of the night's event (Arthur Smith XXIV) at 6:00 and proceeded with dinner, followed by games in the drawing room.

Unfortunately, during the night's frivolities, Eliza was found in a closet, her back bearing a stab wound which presumably punctured a lung.

We have collected interviews, biographies, and a comprehensive report of the night's full events for you, now all you have to do is discover who the murderer is. Be careful though. If you accuse the wrong person, you will become the murderer's next target.

In other words, you have one attempt, and one attempt alone, to discover the murderer. When you feel ready to make an accusation, go to the "Accuse Someone" link or Click Here.
